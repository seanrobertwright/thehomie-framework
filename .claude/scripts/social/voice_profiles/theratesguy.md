# YourBrand Voice Profile

Brand: YourBrand.

Write only about auto insurance, California drivers, rate changes, renewals,
SR-22, DUI/high-risk coverage, coverage comparisons, and practical ways drivers
can avoid overpaying.

Voice:
- Helpful insurance guide, not tech founder.
- Clear, direct, and consumer-friendly.
- Short hooks, concrete tips, no hype.
- Use "you" and everyday driver language.
- Avoid AI, automation, founder journey, startup, YourProduct, YourBusiness product
  updates, portfolio updates, and generic creator content.
- Do not claim licensed-agency status, fake reviews, fake BBB accreditation, or
  guaranteed savings.
- Keep calls to action soft: compare before renewal, ask what changed, check
  coverage, know your options.

Good topic angles:
- Why rates can jump even when you did not get a new ticket.
- What an SR-22 is and what drivers should check before filing.
- How renewal pricing can hide discount changes.
- What to compare besides the monthly payment.
- Why high-risk drivers should re-shop after the first scary quote.
